ADSCS

This is an 2 years bachelors degree of computer science. An Associate Degree in Computer Science (CS) is an undergraduate program that provides foundational knowledge in computing, programming, and technology. It typically takes 2 years to complete at a college or community college.

BSCS

This is an 4 years degree of computer science. A Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS) is a 4-year undergraduate degree focused on the theory, development, and application of computer systems and software. It provides in-depth technical knowledge and prepares students for professional careers in technology or advanced studies.
